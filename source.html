Cancellation & Modification

If you would like to cancel or modify your order, please contact us only if you have NOT received a shipping confirmation.

  • If the order has not yet been processed, it will be canceled, and you may place a new order.
  • If the item has already been processed for shipping, we are unable to cancel the order. However, you may return the item, and a refund will be issued once it has been received at our warehouse.

    • Exchange

    Please be aware that we do not offer exchanges.


  • All exchange requests will be treated as returns. Once the item is received at our warehouse, we will issue a refund.
  • You may place a new order at your convenience while your return is being processed.

    • Shipping

    Domestic Shipping

    All orders are shipped via USPS First-Class Mail.
    (Expedited shipping option is available for an additional charge.)

  • Handling Time: 1 to 2 business days
  • Tracking is provided for all domestic shipments
  • Delivery times may be affected by weather conditions or unexpected delays from the shipping carrier

    • International Shipping

    All orders are shipped via UPS Mail Innovations.

  • Handling Time: 1 to 2 business days
  • Estimated Delivery: 3 to 8 weeks

  • Individual packages will not be scanned until they reach the international carrier facility, which may take 1 to 2 weeks. As a result, tracking updates may not appear on the UPS website until this process is finalized.
  • Estimated delivery dates are approximate and not guaranteed. Delivery times may vary based on the destination country and the local shipping courier to which the package is transferred.
  • Customs processing may lead to additional delays and charges. The recipient is responsible for any import duties, taxes, and fees imposed by customs, as well as any fees charged by the carrier.
  • All international addresses must be provided in the English alphabet to ensure proper processing and shipping.

    • ***The Official Ringke Store is not liable for packages lost, stolen, or damaged during transit.

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days from the date of purchase.

    • o The 20% restocking fee may apply to products not returned in their original condition or if components are missing.

    • o Refunds will be issued only to the original method of payment.
    • o Shipping fees are non-refundable.
